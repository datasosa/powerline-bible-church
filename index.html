Rooted in Faith, Growing in Community

Powerline Bible Church, also known as Wisdom House, is a vibrant and dynamic community located in Lagos, Nigeria. Founded with a commitment to the undiluted Word of Faith, we strive to create a place where everyone can find a family, grow spiritually, and live a life of purpose.
