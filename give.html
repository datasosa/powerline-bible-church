Giving FAQs

Giving is an act of worship and obedience to God. Your giving supports the ministry's work, helps spread the gospel, and enables us to serve our community. Most importantly, it demonstrates trust in God as our provider and opens the door for His blessings in your life (Malachi 3:10-12).

Tithes are 10% of your income, given as worship to God (Leviticus 27:30). Offerings are gifts given above the tithe, expressing gratitude and supporting specific needs (Exodus 35:4-5). Both are important but serve different purposes in your spiritual growth.

For online payments, you'll automatically receive an email receipt. For bank transfers, send your payment details to finance@powerlinebible.org. For cash/check donations, request a receipt from the church office. All annual giving statements are provided each January for tax purposes.
